About

The sport is an activity that the human being carries out mainly with recreational objectives although in some cases it can become the profession of a person if it is dedicated intensively to it and perfects its technique and its results permanently. Sport is basically a physical activity that brings the body into operation and that takes it out of its resting state against which it is normally found.

The importance of sport is that it allows the person to exercise their body to keep it in a good physical level as well as allowing them to relax, relax, disregard the routine, release tension and also have fun. We understand by sport all those physical activities in which the body enters into some type of exercise or movement.
